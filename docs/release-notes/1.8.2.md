# Zocial 1.8.2

*Released 2026-06-12*

A ground-up overhaul of notifications, a quick word-filter shortcut, and more reliable translation. (This is the published release that carries the notification system; the untagged 1.7.1 / 1.8.0 / 1.8.1 increments shipped as part of it.)

## 🔔 Reworked notification system

The centrepiece of this release. Notifications have been reimagined from the ground up.

- Web Push as the primary mechanism — you're now reliably notified even when the tab is closed or Zocial runs as a background app (PWA). Rich notifications with real content (who, what, a preview) instead of a generic "N new notifications".
- Act straight from the notification — reply to mentions with boost/favourite buttons directly from the OS popup.
- One clear setting — the two formerly confusingly similar blocks are now a single "Notify me on this device" switch, with the per-type options beneath it and hover explanations.
- One-time opt-in dialog at login — in-app notifications stay on by default; Zocial asks once about OS notifications (never pushy).
- No more duplicate popups — while the app is open and visible, no disruptive OS popup appears; you just hear the sound and see the notification in the app.
- Dedicated "Notification Sounds" section (on by default, can be turned off) — and the sound follows your in-app filter: a type you've hidden makes no sound either.

**Robustness & UX polish (v1.8.2)**

- Self-healing push subscription — if the subscription is silently lost (e.g. after a service-worker update), Zocial re-registers it automatically on the next load — with your remembered per-type settings, without you having to open settings.
- Clean state when permission is revoked — if you block notifications in the browser, the switch correctly flips to "off" instead of falsely showing "on".
- Re-prompt after re-login — when you log back in, you get the opt-in question again (in line with logout resetting your notification state) — unless you've already made the browser-permission decision.
- Fixed a settings-page crash and cleaner error messages (instead of empty errors on mobile).

⚠️ **Note:** Web Push needs server support (Mastodon ✓, GoToSocial from v0.18). On older servers, the foreground notification remains as a fallback. Push on Firefox for Android is currently unreliable and under investigation.

## 🔇 Word-filter shortcut

- New "Add word filter" entry in every post's "···" menu — filter words right where you encounter them, instead of digging through settings.

## 🌍 More reliable translation

- Accurate detection of unsupported languages (including mixed-language posts), so untranslatable posts are no longer mistaken for "already in your language" and you get a clear message instead of a confusing result.
