# Zocial 1.9.2

## 🐛 Fixes

- **Unfollowing now removes that account's old posts from your home timeline — and from your lists.** Previously their already-loaded posts lingered (Zocial's timeline cache only ever *adds*, never removes, so a refresh didn't drop them — they only aged out over time). Now an unfollow clears them immediately from your home feed and your lists (you can only list accounts you follow, so unfollowing drops them there too), both on screen and from the local cache, matching what the server already does. New posts from the account had already stopped arriving on unfollow; this is only about the old cached ones. Boosts of their posts by people you still follow are kept.
- **Blocking clears them everywhere.** Since a block means you don't want to see anything from that account, their old cached posts are removed from **all** your timelines (home, local, federated, hashtags, lists, profiles) — not just home — immediately and from the local cache.

## 🧹 Internal

- Cleaner logs: transient network failures during follow/unfollow/block/remove-follower (and a failed account-list refresh) are now logged as warnings, not red errors, matching how the timeline already classifies network noise.
