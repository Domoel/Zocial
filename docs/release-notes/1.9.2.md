# Zocial 1.9.2

## 🐛 Fixes

- **Unfollowing now removes that account's old posts from your home timeline.** Previously their already-loaded posts lingered (Zocial's timeline cache only ever *adds*, never removes, so a refresh didn't drop them — they only aged out over time). Now an unfollow clears them immediately, both on screen and from the local cache, matching what you'd expect (and what the server already does to your home feed). New posts from the account had already stopped arriving on unfollow; this is only about the old cached ones. Boosts of their posts by people you still follow are kept.
- **Blocking clears them everywhere.** Since a block means you don't want to see anything from that account, their old cached posts are removed from **all** your timelines (home, local, federated, hashtags, lists, profiles) — not just home — immediately and from the local cache.
